How to view mathematics

Let's start off with a bit of philosophical musing on mathematics. What is maths? Bertrand Russell and Whitehead attempted an answer a century ago and ended up with several kilos of writing and not much else that either philosophy or mathematics remembers them by. The right question is what our attitude towards mathematics should be. How should we relate to it as programmers? To see it as akin to something holy or the ultimate recognition of competence does not seem to be something senior developers would agree with. Instead they favour the view that you should see it as something subsidiary to the programming enterprise as a whole. A means, a tool. Perhaps it would not be too farfetched to call it a technology. Just like being able to aim well when throwing an object was a technology for early humans. Or the ability to direct attention at an object being pointed at.

Random Module

The function randint() works well for producing random numbers but it has the disadvantage that some of those numbers will repeat themselves. In come choice() and sample().

choice(sequence) chooses a random number from a number and returns it.
